Frequently Asked Questions

Don't see your question below? Get in touch !

How long is the free trial on the Free plan?

The Free starter plan is free for as long as Font Awesome is around and has plans. There's no trial period at all. You can add Font Awesome Free icons to your heart's content, and we won't pull the rug out from under you. Oh, and we never sell your data - it's really free.

What's a Pageview?

Each time one of your pages with a Font Awesome Kit is viewed, a pageview is counted. Pageviews are counted on a monthly basis. Most sites fit comfortably in the 1M pageviews included in our Pro plan.

Where and how can I upload my own icons?

You can upload your own SVG icons to any Pro Kit and serve them reliably right alongside your Font Awesome icons. Kits are the easiest and most performant way to use Font Awesome on the web, and now with Kit Icon Upload, they're also the most extendable.

How do I manage and add seats?

When you sign up for a plan, you can adjust how many seats you will need - considering your organization and clients. If you're already on a plan, you can adjust seats from your account settings.
